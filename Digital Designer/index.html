My History

Alyssa Jasper is a freelance digital designer with over 10+ years of experience in advertising & tech.

My Vision

I’m a digital designer that focuses on UI/UX for the web. I help clients visualize their brand’s best. I don’t just create logos but focus on uncovering the strategy you and your business needs to achieve success. The core of my approach is communication. I want to get to know you and your brand and what makes you tick, what keeps you up at night and where you wish you were in the future. We then create a new vision and execute on it, together.
